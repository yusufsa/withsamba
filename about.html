Who are we?

A team of MIT and IDEO designers, researchers, and engineers. We've previously started a university, a coding bootcamp, and worked on product development with some of the world's leading companies.

Why Samba?

We want to create a community inspired by the best places we've worked.

We hope that Samba will become a place where anyone can work on projects as part of an inspiring, mixed-age and mixed-ability community. Where many paths and diverse styles are celebrated. Where creative exploration and 'hard fun' are paths for self-discovery and contributing to the world.

Our work builds on research from MIT's Media Lab and Lifelong Kindergarten Research Group.
