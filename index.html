Why Samba?

Everyone has the potential to do amazing things. But too often, we're not in the right context.

Maybe you're trying to figure out what to work on, how to get started, or how to build momentum.
‍
Maybe you're just looking for people to explore with.
‍
That's why we created Samba.

What is Samba?

Samba is a community where members explore their interests together.

We’ll support you with weekly reflection rituals, co-working sessions, and regular demo days.

Samba's model is based on the magic behind the best research groups, startups, and design studios.

We've tested Samba at MIT and now want to bring it to you.

Who is Samba for?

You might be early in exploring your interests. Or already deep with certain projects.

Regardless of where you are in your journey, you're excited to learn by doing, ready to embrace failure, and eager to collaborate with others.
